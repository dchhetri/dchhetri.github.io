Optimize Operations & Reduce Overhead

Alfred empowers guests to take control of their experience—from ordering and inquiries to service requests—without needing constant attention from waitstaff. This autonomy not only improves customer satisfaction but also allows you to operate efficiently with fewer staff on the floor, especially during off-peak hours. The result? Reduced labor costs without compromising service quality.

Voice-Activated Assistant

Alfred listens and responds like a seasoned maître d’. With intuitive voice interaction, guests can make requests, hear daily specials, or get assistance without lifting a finger. This AI elevates convenience to conversational hospitality.

Smart Menu Navigation

With visual highlights and dietary filters, Alfred presents the menu like a personal sommelier—customized, clear, and intelligent. Guests glide through choices hands-free, discovering dishes tailored to their taste and needs.

Empowered Waitstaff

By offloading routine tasks to Alfred, human waitstaff are freed to focus on creating an atmosphere of warmth and connection. It’s not about replacing service—it’s about enhancing it, giving staff more time to be present, not pressured.

Real-Time Table Insights

Admins gain instant visibility into the dining floor with Alfred’s live dashboard. Monitor table occupancy, guest engagement, wait times, service requests, and satisfaction signals—all in one intelligent, color-coded interface.
